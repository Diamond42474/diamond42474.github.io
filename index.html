About Me

Interests

I am an embedded systems enthusiast based in Arizona, deeply captivated by the intricacies of low-level automation and advanced avionics. My journey in the world of technology has led me to a profound fascination with harnessing the full potential of embedded systems. Whether it's developing software for unmanned aerial vehicles with thrust vectoring capabilities or delving into the complexities of avionics systems, I am driven by a relentless passion for pushing the boundaries of what's possible. Currently pursuing a degree in software development, my ultimate goal is to contribute my expertise to the aerospace industry, specializing in the art of low-level programming. With a solid foundation in C, as well as proficiency in a range of programming languages and technologies, I am well-prepared to tackle the most challenging endeavors in the field of embedded systems and advanced avionics.

Achievements

I have consistently demonstrated a commitment to excellence in both my academic and technical endeavors. Earning a place on the GCU President's List, with a GPA exceeding 3.9, reflects my dedication to maintaining the highest standards of academic performance. Additionally, I am among the privileged few students recognized with the FIRST Robotics FRC Dean's List award, an honor bestowed upon only a select few out of thousands each year. As the head programmer for my robotics team, I developed a sophisticated Deep Neural Network that predicted the performance of other robots in competitions, showcasing my technical prowess and strategic thinking. Furthermore, my foray into hacking and website vulnerabilities, despite being relatively new to the field, led me to secure first place in a GCU Penetration Testing Competition focused on OWASP standards. My approach involved leveraging my programming knowledge to automate tasks and uncover common vulnerabilities, highlighting my adaptability and problem-solving skills. These achievements not only underscore my academic excellence but also exemplify my practical skills, creativity, and unwavering commitment to continuous learning.
